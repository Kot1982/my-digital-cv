Web application for accounting of your funds. A convenient application for entering income and expenses. You can also analyze your expenses for any period.

Role: Scrum Master. Main responsibilities includes: organization of team work, communication with team members, monitoring deadlines for tasks, organization of meetings.

Using technologies:

  • JavaScript, React, REST API, Parcel
  • Node.js, MongoDB, Express, Mongoose
